Admin Help & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to add/edit users?
    Go to the Users panel. Click “Add User” to register a new one or choose an existing user to update their profile.
  • How to manage attendance?
    Click the Attendance tab. Filter by batch/date, then mark or update attendance status as needed.
  • How to generate reports?
    Use the Reports module. Select the report type (Attendance, Finance, etc.), then generate and export.
  • How to assign batches/trainers?
    Open Courses > Assign. Choose the course, assign batches, and select available trainers.
  • How to export data?
    In tables, click the “Export” button (top-right). Choose Excel or PDF format to save your data.
